Examples

  • Open Web Steel Joist
    • Models a parametric open web steel joist with back-to-back angle chords.
  • Bays of Joists
    • Creates a building roof structure with 250 open web steel joists arranged in 5 bays of 50 joists each, spaced 8 feet apart.
  • Bigger Bays of Joists
    • Creates a building roof structure with 1000 open web steel joists arranged in 10 bays of 100 joists each, spaced 8 feet apart.
  • Taller & Bigger Bays of Joists
    • Creates a multi-story building structure with 5000 open web steel joists arranged in 5 floors of 10 bays with 100 joists each. Story height: 32 feet.
  • What If The Joists Are All Different
    • Creates the same multi-story building, but treats each joist as unique to test performance.
  • What If The Joists Are All Different But Fast Too
    • Creates the same multi-story building, but treats each joist as unique to test performance AND uses a faster algorithm for rendering.
